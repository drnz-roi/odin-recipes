Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and a rich mascarpone cream, dusted with cocoa powder.

It's elegant, indulgent, and requires no baking — perfect for impressing guests.

Ingredients

  • 300g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 500g mascarpone cheese
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 cups strong espresso, cooled
  • 2 tbsp rum or marsala wine (optional)
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Steps

  1. Beat egg yolks with sugar until pale and creamy.
  2. Mix in mascarpone until smooth.
  3. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Mix espresso with rum if using.
  5. Dip ladyfingers briefly in espresso and layer in a dish.
  6. Spread half the mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers.
  7. Repeat with another layer of ladyfingers and cream.
  8. Dust with cocoa powder, refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving.
