Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's hearty, comforting, and perfect for feeding a crowd.

Best served fresh out of the oven with a side of garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef with garlic, drain fat, add marinara sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Layer: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat.
  6. Top with remaining sauce and parmesan.
  7. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 more minutes.
  8. Let rest 15 minutes before serving.
